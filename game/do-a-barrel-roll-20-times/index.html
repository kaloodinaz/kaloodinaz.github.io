Do a Barrel Roll 20 times

What is the Do a Barrel Roll Game?

The 'Do a Barrel Roll' game is a playful Easter egg created by Google that mimics a classic maneuver from the game Star Fox 64. When you search for "Do a Barrel Roll" on Google, the entire search page spins 360 degrees in a barrel roll motion. This quirky feature has been a fan favorite since its launch in 2011.

How to Play 'Do a Barrel Roll 20 Times'

To take on the challenge of completing 20 barrel rolls, follow these steps:

  1. Open Google Search in your web browser.
  2. Type "Do a Barrel Roll 20 Times" into the search bar and press Enter.
  3. Watch as the screen performs the barrel roll repeatedly, up to 20 times.

If your browser doesn’t support the feature, you can use third-party tools or scripts designed to replicate the effect.

Why is Do a Barrel Roll So Popular?

The popularity of this Easter egg stems from its simplicity and nostalgic connection to Star Fox 64. Gamers and non-gamers alike enjoy its humorous and lighthearted nature, making it one of Google’s most memorable tricks.

Tips for Enjoying 'Do a Barrel Roll 20 Times'
